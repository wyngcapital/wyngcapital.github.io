About WYNG

WYNG is acronym for a ancient chinese words ‘外柔內剛’: Soft and gentle outlook with tough and strong mind. Like our name WYNG team is to work gently with people from industry to network and connect but when it comes to investing in a project, we are willing to use our tough mind to help projects its full potential.

WYNG members are consist of various experts in block chain industry with different backgrounds with multiple years of experience. With their knowledge of industry and commitment to help projects WYNG will help projects to achieve their goals.
